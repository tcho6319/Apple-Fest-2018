Our Craft Show

AppleFest's Craft Show displays the variety of goods made by the local craftsmen of Ithaca! We have listed some of the different items, accepted methods of payment, and the price range of the crafts.

Collapsible Wooden Baskets

A beautiful craft item is the collapsible wooden basket. When in "basket-form", the baskets show off their special shape, some of which include a swan and a flower. The cool part is that they can flatten completely!

Confirmed method of payment: Cash

Price Range: Around $50 per item

Handmade Glass Charms and Pendents

A quaint and charming craft item are the glass charms and pendents. They come in all shapes, including a musical note, turtle, flower, star, and so much more!

Confirmed method of payment: Cash

Unconfirmed Price Range: Around $10 per item

Stones and Crystals

Stressed about upcoming prelims? Pick up a worry stone or rose quartz crystals to soothe your mind.

Confirmed method of payment: Cash

Unconfirmed Price Range: Around $5 per item
