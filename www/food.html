Our Food Vendors

AppleFest showcases the variety of tasty dishes that Ithaca has to offer from farm to market! We have listed some of the food vendors, their accepted methods of payment, and the price range of their dishes.

Little Grey Bakery

Little Grey Bakery has all kinds of baked treats, including pies, muffins, bread, and so much more!

Confirmed method of payment: Cash

Price Range: About $4 and up per item

Littletree Orchards LLC

Littletree Orchards LLC is a family farm that offers a variety of apple products, including apples, apple cider, apple cider donuts, apple cider vinegar, and for a limited time - pear-apple cider!

Confirmed methods of payment: Cash, Check, Square, Credit Cards with a $10 minimum

Price Range: $4 to $12 per item

NOTE: For all you busy bees, Littletree Orchards offers prepackaged 1/2 dozen and dozen donuts so that you can skip the long line!

Macarollin

Macarollin has fun types of gourmet mac and cheese, including Classic Mac, Buffalo Chicken Mac, and Lobster Mac.

Confirmed methods of payment: Cash

Price Range: $8 to $14 per item

Some Other Vendors: Luna Inspired Street Food, Sangam Kitchen, Thai Basil, and so much more!
